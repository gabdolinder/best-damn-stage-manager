Stockholm Pride Artist & Guest list management

Welcome to the system where we manage tickets for our artists and their guests


HELP! The system isn't working as it should!

First of all, check with your shift lead or team lead if they know how to solve the problem.
If they can't help you then please don't hesitate to contact either Peter or Gabby in the stage team. (your shift or team lead should have the number)



FAQ

Question Answer
I accidently checked in a ticket and need it reversed, what do I do? Contact Gabby or Peter who can reverse it. They need to know who it was and which ticket.
I can't find the artist or guest, but they claim they should be in the system The person who sent the names in has to get in touch with their contact at Stockholm Pride to get it sorted out
The person claims that they're just added as a +1 on the list but not with their name specified At Stockolm Pride we don't do +1 tickets without a name on them. The person in front of you is most likely trying to be sneaky and get a free ticket. Ask them to talk to their contact person who got them added to the list.
